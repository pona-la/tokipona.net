title: Can learning Toki Pona improve your mental health?

published: true

# simple: 
---

Not exactly. Nothing about Toki Pona inherently has any effect on your mental
health, whether positive or negative. This is a common misconception built on
the way Toki Pona was described in the 2000s and 2010s, as a tool for
simplifying your thoughts.

It is possible for Toki Pona to improve your mental health if you approach it as
a means for doing so, but then, it would not be any more effective at improving
your mental health than picking up any other hobby.
