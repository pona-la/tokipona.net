# simple: 
---


The figure you’ll hear most often is 120 words, but most speakers use somewhere around 130. However, every individual speaker will have a slightly different list of words they use, which makes pinning down a specific number tricky. You'll also find some speakers using a few more words than 130, and some using less than 120. On occasion, you'll find a speaker who uses many more words, but this is uncommon.
