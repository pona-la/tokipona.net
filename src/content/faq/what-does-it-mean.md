## What does “Toki Pona” even mean?

In Toki Pona, _toki_ means 'language, communication, speech,' while _pona_ means 'good, positive quality.' Together, they mean something like 'good language' or 'pleasant communication.'

When speaking Toki Pona, depending on the context, a speaker can use _toki pona_ to refer to the language itself, or to good communication more generally. After your friend gives a speech, you encourage them by saying, "_sina toki pona!_", or "You spoke well!"
