Pinning down an exact number of Toki Pona speakers is hard. It depends on who
counts as a speaker, who's willing to fill out surveys, and who can even be
found to be counted. That said, there is some data to fill out our estimates:

- [The 2024 community census](https://tokiponacensus.github.io/results2024/) had
  ~2,000 respondents, and ~1,600 of those respondents reported to know Toki
  Pona.
- In 2023-2025, ilo Muni shows
  [600-1,100 authors active monthly](https://ilo.muni.la/?query=e%2C+a%2C+mi%2C+pona%2C+toki%2C+li%2C+o%2C+ni%2C+ala%2C+jan%2C+la%2C+sina&scale=abs&field=authors&start=1690848000&end=1754006400&unit=month)
  and
  [3,100-4,300 authors active annually](https://ilo.muni.la/?query=e%2C+a%2C+mi%2C+pona%2C+toki%2C+li%2C+o%2C+ni%2C+ala%2C+jan%2C+la%2C+sina&scale=abs&field=authors&start=1690848000&end=1754006400&unit=year)
  among the twelve most commonly used Toki Pona words.
- In May 2026, the three largest Toki Pona communities have
  [33,000](https://reddit.com/r/tokipona), [20,000](https://discord.gg/mapona),
  and [8,000](https://discord.com/invite/kama-sona-969386329513295872) members,
  with monthly readership among them ranging from 1,500 to 7,500 users, and
  monthly participation ranging from 150 to 600 users.

All of these numbers have caveats and complexities, but between them all, you
could argue that there are anywhere from ~1,500 to ~30,000 Toki Pona speakers.
ilo Muni's numbers provide the most confident estimate, as they are derived from
Toki Pona use in
[many different communities](https://ilo.muni.la/blog/about/#where-is-the-data-from).
This leaves us with a confident range of **3,000 to 4,000 Toki Pona speakers**.

But this number is still short of the true number of Toki Pona speakers. Some
may participate in its online communities in ways that are hard to measure, like
voice chat, direct message, or even virtual reality. Others may rarely or never
participate in Toki Pona's online communities, instead preferring to journal in
private, or speak Toki Pona among in-person friends.

They are as much Toki Pona speakers as anyone in the online communities, and by
some measures, they could reasonably _double_ the total number of speakers;
after all, Toki Pona has reached
[over a million people](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kSl-iF9ZRLk) on YouTube,
[more than once](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E4y7tf3VJAM)! However, there is
only indirect evidence and anecdotes available to estimate any further, so the
prior estimate stands.
