## Contact Us

**External inquiries may be directed to [kulupu@tokipona.net](mailto:kulupu@tokipona.net).**

The team monitoring this inbox is a growing collective of Toki Pona speakers, each knowledgeable about different aspects of the community and facets of the language. Together, we serve as a kind of information desk and directory to the community.  

**General Toki Pona Help:** We will do our best to connect you with the information you are looking for, whether you have questions  about the language itself, about navigating the community, about how to learn the language or where to find certain kinds of resources, or anything else we haven't foreseen to outline on this page.
