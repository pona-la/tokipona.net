I picked up Toki Pona on a whim (mostly as a way to procrastinate the thousand
other things that needed to get done) after seeing a video about it. What
started as "Oh let me just learn a few words for fun" turned into an utter and
complete obsession with the language. While I am far from "fluent," I find
myself using the language all the time for notetaking, personal writing,
thinking, chatting with folks in the Toki Pona community, and driving my
girlfriend crazy.

Is it, strictly speaking, "useful"? Not really. But it's darn fun.
