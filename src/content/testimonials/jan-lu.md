If I remember correctly, I first learned Toki Pona from jan Misali's 12 Days of Toki Pona video series, and joined online communities a year or so after first learning it, around 2022-2023. 

Throughout the 4 or so years that I have known it, Toki Pona has helped me get through a lot personally. When at some of my lowest points these past few years, I would journal in Toki Pona to help me to better understand my thoughts (wants, needs, emotions, etc). During these times I would look forward to spending my extremely limited remaining social energy in Toki Pona spaces online, often just lurking and reading. I had a little sort of personal mantra type thing, *mi awen lon*, meaning something like "I keep existing", or "I'm still here". At one point, I would write this phrase (in *sitelen pona*) using Sharpie marker on my arm daily, and it would help me center myself and keep going when I was really struggling. 

I am not as active in the community as I would like to be, and I would like to become more involved in the future.
