toki pona is my language. being a toki pona speaker is just as much a part of my identity as being an english speaker or being an urdu speaker. toki pona is for speaking in. it's for meeting people and talking with them and making friends. so many of my friends in real life i met through or with the help of toki pona, and toki pona has greatly contributed to my romantic relationships as well. day-to-day, i use toki pona for talking to people, and i also help teach it to new learners.
