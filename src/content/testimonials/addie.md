Toki Pona has been a fun little side project for me. It's fun to read and write *sitelen pona*, it's like a secret language that only a select few people understand. Even though I'm not very active in the community, you don't have to be to learn the language and enjoy it. 

Toki Pona has also weirdly helped me with learning other languages. I started to learn Estonian and a good amount of Toki Pona words are the same as Estonian words (*sina, nimi, kala*) which has helped me with that language and it's also a bit of familiarity to hold on to while I'm learning. Toki Pona has always felt like home.
