Toki Pona has become my favorite conlang (out of two) and helped me a lot when I suffered from depression. I used it on my diaries and tea reviews last year, solely for the purpose of using this tiny language. But as time went by I began to feel uncomfortable about its oversimplicity at times and reduced its use on my daily life. This being said, I still love Toki Pona and would recommend it to my friends, should they be interested.
