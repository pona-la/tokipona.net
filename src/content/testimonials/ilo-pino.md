toki pona is presented as an artistic, philosophical language and i have to agree. increasing my fluency has motivated me to tend to my artistic projects, and in turn, strengthened my bonds with the people i learned alongside with.

toki pona has been revolutionary in the way it has helped me with my disorganized thinking; thanks to the large community of speakers, i have found ways to break down my thought patterns in a way that is more easily communicable to others. i journal in it, i take project notes in it, i conceptualize my identity in it.
