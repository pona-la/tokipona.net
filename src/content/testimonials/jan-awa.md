for me, toki pona is mostly for detaching myself from strong feelings and
slowing down! when i write in korean or english i tend to write and understand
things quickly. but i tend to take more time and thinking to write in toki pona,
both because i am less proficient in it and the small vocabulary makes me think
what i really need to express.

it also makes me judge my writing less based on word choice, plus other people
can't read what i wrote so i don't need to worry about other people judging me
either. this is why i often write my most cringe writings in toki pona! (like a
story about a kid in a boarding school who becomes best friends with a raccoon
and pranks people in the school) also sitelen pona is very cute and handwriting
sitelen pona into my notebook is really satisfying.

as for the community, it inspired me to get into linguistics and conlanging!
it's also taught me that queer people exist and some of them are really cool
people. the downside is i doomscrolled too many toki pona
memes/videos/literature lol
