For me, Toki Pona is a way of artistic expression. It also allows me a way to communicate my thoughts clearly and concisely via the limited vocabulary.

In my opinion, Toki Pona is for having fun! I also see it as like a challenge to compress information. Toki Pona has affected my life by giving me another creative outlet to express myself in. It's a hobby that I've dedicated the past year or so getting better at! Toki Pona gives me purpose.

I haven't really used Toki Pona much in my day to day as the number of Tokiponists located in my continent is miniscule compared to places like North America and Europe. One thing I have used it for is to pass the time while waiting for stuff or after exams, waiting for the bell to ring.
