*toki pona* has been a way for us to connect with people. it not only has served as an alternate language to use among our friends, but also to reach new friends, some of which may not even speak our language. while we were learning, the Project Babel and MA TOKI PONA VR group provided a safe space to immerse in the language. they had taught us that *toki pona* is more than just a personal project, or a way to think differently, its also a living language with colorful and bright speakers. our shared interest in the language has allowed us to connect to one of the most inclusive communities on the web.
