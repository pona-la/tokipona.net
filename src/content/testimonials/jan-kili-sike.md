Toki Pona has been such a fun and comfortable language to learn. After many
years of difficulty on and off with trying to acquire a second language,
learning Toki Pona through comprehensible input was finally the missing piece
that made everything start to make sense!

I feel so much more confident in learning new languages now, and I love chatting
to folks in Toki Pona. I also really want to use it in one of my games in the
future!
