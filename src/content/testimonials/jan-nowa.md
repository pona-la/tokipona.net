toki pona taught me how language actually works. Sure, I spoke English, but never stopped to think about the *why* and the *how* of language. How come English still Englishes when I use it as a verb? Why is that not how English works? In toki pona that's completely normal. It was these sorts of critical quetions that changed how I viewed language, and made any form of creative expression through it so much richer to me.
