I have been unable to summon the focus to study any natural language to the
point of fluency. Toki Pona has been my lifeline, my avenue to finally becoming
multilingual. The gradual process of discovering the creative ways that speakers
have found to express thoughts and feelings and describe worldly phenomena with
such a limited toolset has been rewarding as well as tremendous fun. Reading
stories and listening to lectures in Toki Pona serves as mental exercise that
I'm very grateful for
