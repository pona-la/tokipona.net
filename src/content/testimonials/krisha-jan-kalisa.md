I am not a fluent speaker. I don't use _toki pona_ on a day to day basis, nor am
I heavily engaged in the community. However, as the only conlang I've learned, I
do consider it a not insignificant part of myself. I translated a song, and its
structure helped me come up with worldbuilding concepts.

_toki pona_ for me is an avenue for playing with language. Its simple
phonotactics sound cute and it is fun and creatively engaging to come up with
translations and definitions using its limited vocabulary.
