I love _toki pona_. I learned it years ago, and it's a lovely microcosm of the
language-learning experience. I have had quite a few years of experience
learning languages, and it's really interesting to be able to experience that
which normally takes years of consistent effort happen over a single weekend.

The community is super sweet, and it's always a really fun topic to talk to
anyone about. I've never met anyone who couldn't at least find some level of
appreciation for the language. And trust me, I've talked about it with MANY
people.
