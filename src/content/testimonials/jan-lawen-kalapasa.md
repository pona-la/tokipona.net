Toki Pona has been a fun way for me to think differently about words.
Particularly, I’ve enjoyed practicing translation of the Bible and some of my
favorite stories, especially where I’ve been able to collaborate and chat with
others about our different translation styles. Plus, it’s a neat little method
for note-taking during seminars!
