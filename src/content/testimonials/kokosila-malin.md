As influenced by multiple conlangs as many Tokiponists are, I treat Toki Pona as an escape hatch to perplexed real life. Toki Pona is simple but expressive enough for a weekend project, so Tokiponists are engaged to make something occasionally, making the community rather creative, vivid, and inclusive.

When I talk to myself (sometimes aloud or else I cannot think deep) I jumble Toki Pona and other tongues, helping myself think flexibly.
