I had learnt Toki Pona from a friend's suggestion in August 2025, and immediately I became hooked. Not only did I see it as a vast hobby with many ways to express myself, I saw a community of speakers who was willing to teach me and nurture a skill within me that I genuinely thought I could not gain. I saw a language that gave me ways to talk to people who I would otherwise have nothing in common with, and grow a little bit closer to those around me.

This language gave me friends and connections which changed my life for the better, and throughout so much of that was a language which felt personal. Toki Pona to me feels like a language which builds connections, a language that lets me come to understand those around me in a friendly and positive manner.
