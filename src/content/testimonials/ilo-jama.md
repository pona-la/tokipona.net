I ultimately think that the purpose of Toki Pona is, first and foremost, to have
fun. And it has been fun and continues to be to me! I'm a shy person, but Toki
Pona has motivated me to share my works with others. Watching the Toki Pona
community expand lately has been so cool, even if I've only been taking the
conlang seriously for the past eight-ish months or so.

I initially began learning the language as a joke for a translation mod I was
working on, but nowadays, I feel comfortable saying that I'm at an intermediate
level! I use it almost every day, whether it's me writing down my dreams or
translating more video games into Toki Pona. I truly think it helped me better
understand how other languages work, too. I'm even more interested in
linguistics now!
