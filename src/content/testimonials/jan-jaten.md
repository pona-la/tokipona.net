Toki Pona is a way to slow down and really think about what things mean. It’s another perspective of the world. Every language provides a different perspective but not every language boils things down to their base elements. Toki Pona does this for me. It simplifies things to a comfortable basic element. Uniquely, not only does it simplify things but it also provides a fun challenge. Figuring out how to best explain or describe something clearly in Toki Pona is a fun puzzle for me. I deeply enjoy the challenge.

For me Toki Pona is for fun, meditation and secrecy. It’s often like a game. It stimulates deep thought. And I love talking to my wife in it and not having the people around us understand our conversation.

Toki Pona has helped boost my confidence in learning languages and has been a fun distraction in times of stress. I use it daily for reading, chatting with people online and talking secretly with my wife.
