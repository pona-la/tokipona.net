I originally explored _toki pona_ to get a better understanding of minimalism.
As I started to learn more about the language, I was surprised and elated by the
artistic culture in the _toki pona_ community. People make cool additions to the
language like new beautiful writing systems, but also music, stories, and
playing cards, and video games!

This community is really friendly to new people as well. I never thought I'd get
to speak to people from all over the world, but this community has people from
all over who I can talk to and broaden my horizons that little bit further.
Having a community of really cool creative people has inspired and motivated me
to make things that I might not otherwise because I want to make something cool
for my new friends who are a shockingly similar kind of nerd. If you want to
join the _toki pona_ community as well, maybe you will find that these are your
people too.
