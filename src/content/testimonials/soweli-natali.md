at first, the prospect of learning a language in only a few weeks seemed appealing. but now, it isn't the language by itself that i enjoy; to me, *toki pona* is above all a community. even though i was very immature at the time i found out about it, it did and still does give me a strong sense of belonging. many of my online friends today are ones i ultimately met through *toki pona*!
