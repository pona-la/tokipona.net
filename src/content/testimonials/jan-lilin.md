*toki pona* influenced my studies and my life by quite a bit. It is what sparked my love for learning languages and led to me understanding other languages, most notably Japanese, *much* faster than an average learner thanks to certain transferable principles that *toki pona* made easy to understand - not to mention how the language made me more interesting to people! I hold a great appreciation for my past self deciding to learn it, although I don't really navigate any communities anymore and thus don't really use the language in my day to day life.
