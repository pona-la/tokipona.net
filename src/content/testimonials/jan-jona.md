Toki Pona is not just any ordinary conlang. To me, it gave me a way to reach out to others who have the same interests as me. It gives me a lively community and something to fill my day. I often use it to express myself and compress my thoughts in a way English has never given me, and now I can distill my thoughts from the white noise. It gave me a filter, a gate, and a community of like minds, that I hope future learners will like as well.
