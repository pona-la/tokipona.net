There are many things that are much harder to say in Toki Pona: linear algebra,
rules of mahjong, the linguistics of the language itself. But my god there is no
better language for flirting with gay people on the internet.

I have known this language for almost six years, since I was 14. It has given me
so, so much. Some of my best friends I know through the Toki Pona community;
some of my greatest projects are made in Toki Pona. But most importantly, it has
allowed me to speak simply, without having to stress over my word choice or
connotations. When you just want to hang with friends, there is no better way to
do it than with the language of good.
