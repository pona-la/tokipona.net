Toki Pona is really special to me. It's a really fun language to learn, and I have recently started to teach it to my girlfriend so we can speak and practice together. For me, Toki Pona is for connecting with people all around the world. I have met people (in Discord, of course) from different continents, and I'm not even close to any of them! If you have nothing to do lately, go for trying to learn Toki Pona! I guarantee you'll have a neat time.
