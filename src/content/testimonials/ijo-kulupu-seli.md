Toki Pona has honestly helped me a lot. Learning a new language is always fun, and I got to meet lots of like-minded people who were also interested in language and fun. Toki Pona is one of those things that you can learn quickly, but takes a while to master. If anything, I'm still a novice: not really a beginner anymore, but still learning every time I speak. *ni li musi a*! (It's fun)!

I don't tend to use Toki Pona that often, but in general, I like trying to translate my thoughts into Toki Pona, breaking them down into simpler terms while still retaining their meaning. It helps organize my very disparate thoughts and, if nothing else, is a fun mental exercise.

Overall, I highly recommend people learn and speak Toki Pona. It's a fun language, an accepting and fun community, and just, well, pona!
