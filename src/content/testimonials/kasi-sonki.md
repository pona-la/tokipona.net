Toki Pona means many things to me. It's a language I can use to learn more about
myself and the world around me with, it's an outlet for social connections and a
great way to make friends with common interests. Toki Pona is for anyone who
wants a different way of communicating, a fun hobby, a chance to learn a
language well, a place to put creative efforts into, and tons of other things!
Toki Pona is not only for fun, it has serious use as a language. This is how I
personally use it. My daily use of Toki Pona is just talking to people using it
just like I would any other language. Just because a language is small does not
mean it isn't effective at making relationships (for me, Toki Pona might even be
better than English for me.) If I did not learn Toki Pona then my life would be
significantly different
