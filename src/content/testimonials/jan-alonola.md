Back when there was the Corona lockdown, I had a lot of free time. I got interested in Toki Pona and learned it. But you really never stop learning. I have made some YouTube videos before, and make a lot of videos on Toki Pona and linguistics. In 2021, we started a magazine called [*lipu tenpo*](https://liputenpo.org/). I still have so much fun working with the same kind of nerds as me on this project. 

I have met so many interesting, creative and in general incredible people through Toki Pona. I would really say, that I have becoma a somewhat more social person through this. I have met *jan pi toki pona* on multiple meetups. I talked to them about my crazy plans and stupid ideas. Day-to-day I write, draw, make music: All using *toki pona*. I always liked expressing myself artistically but Toki Pona helped a lot with that. *pona tawa jan ale!*
