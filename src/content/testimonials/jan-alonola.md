Back when there was the Corona lockdown, I had a lot of free time. I got
interested in Toki Pona and learned it. But you really never stop learning. I
have made some YouTube videos before, and make a lot of videos on Toki Pona and
linguistics. In 2021, we started a magazine called
[_lipu tenpo_](https://liputenpo.org/). I still have so much fun working with
the same kind of nerds as me on this project.

I have met so many interesting, creative and in general incredible people
through Toki Pona. I would really say, that I have becoma a somewhat more social
person through this. I have met _jan pi toki pona_ on multiple meetups. I talked
to them about my crazy plans and stupid ideas. Day-to-day I write, draw, make
music: All using _toki pona_. I always liked expressing myself artistically but
Toki Pona helped a lot with that. _pona tawa jan ale!_
