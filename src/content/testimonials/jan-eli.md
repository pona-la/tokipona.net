I learned about this language on video sharing sites, and copied down the official textbook "Toki Pona: The Language of Good" onto my school notebook. After a week, I mastered this language. That's the beginning. I then taught this language to my friend, and we started cursing each other, such as "sona[sic] ike". When we parted, we said "sina jan pona mi" to each other. I like this language, it's very "pona" to me. Thank you all!*
