Frankly, Toki Pona is more than just a language to me. The community surrounding it is something very special, something you don't really see too much these days. As such, it has affected my life by helping me think more positively and introducing me to new friends. 

(aside: without learning about Toki Pona, I would likely not have my current group of Kerbalist friends!) 

I, of course, mainly use Toki Pona for translation of names, to eventually make a big map of Europe, all in Toki Pona. It has also made me more interested in linguistics as a whole.
