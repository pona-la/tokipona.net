toki pona fundamentally changed the way i view many things, most obviously language (my views of linguistics can be split into before and after i was made aware of it), but also concepts such as gender and numerology. it helped me realize that many of the axioms upon which i based my construct of reality were simply arbitrary, it showed me a degree of freedom i simply couldnt have seen otherwise. at last, learning toki pona was, for me, a practical exercise in how the way one speaks shapes and is shaped by how they view their world.
