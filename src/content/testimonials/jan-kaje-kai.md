I believe that Toki Pona is for people who want a good mental exercise. You want to talk about your interest? Break it down into what it is to you. Toki Pona is a mirror you can use to see how you yourself view the world. 

Toki Pona reignited my interest in linguistics again, bringing my attention back to something that I already held dear. Even then, Toki Pona was never *just* a language to me. It is a community full of people who try their best to support your learning. It is a way for me to find friends. 

Personally, it's also a way to have a shared interest with my dad. Right now, I use Toki Pona in my day to day by playing Minecraft with my partner and studying with my dad. In other words, I use it to get closer to those I love.
