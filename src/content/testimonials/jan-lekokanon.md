toki pona means a lot to me. it helped me gain confidence in learning other languages, such as spanish. with only 120 core words, it gives me the building blocks of basic language learning. it has affected my life in a good way. i now have good friends whom i met in the toki pona community, such as kije Nawili and jan Janpa Onjon. i use toki pona in my day to day life by talking to my tokiponist friends in toki pona. i don't speak it that much, but i still have the information, but sometimes i feel like it is slipping away from me.

so thank you all to the people who've helped me learn the language, teach me the correct grammar and understand how sometimes simplicity is the best thing. thank you.
