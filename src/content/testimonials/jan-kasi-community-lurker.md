*toki pona*: not a language, for me, but a philosophy. 

it taught me about context and what is important, about what I really need to say and don't. I feel more confident explaining and understanding things because fun little language lets me break things into parts.

actually, I'd recommend *toki pona* to everyone regardless of origin. it's helped massively with my learning of french- I've never been great with retaining vocabulary, but now I'm able to get around during, say, a weekend in Montreal, because I know how to use simple words to mean so, so much more.

*toki pona*: a language of understanding.
