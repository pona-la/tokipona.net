Toki Pona is a language that I learned at a time in my life I was starting to
figure out who I was. I wouldn't say it changed my life, but it was part of a
life-changing few years that have continued to propogate.

At this point it's been four or five years. I've changed significantly in that
time and I can't say I've kept up with the language that entire time. Life got a
little harder and my time became more and more cannibalized. But that's what
made Toki Pona possible. It's something I could engage with when I needed, could
examine when I wanted. I'm glad it followed me out of high school and into the
end of my university life, and will continue to follow me throughout my life.
