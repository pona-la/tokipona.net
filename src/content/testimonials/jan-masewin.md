To me, Toki Pona is a fun puzzle, a discreet way to talk in public, and the home of a wonderfully queer and neurodivergent community. Communicating in Toki Pona has a big cognitive load on me compared to other languages, so I use it mostly for face-to face chats, making art, and context-heavy translations, stretching the vocab and grammar where I can to be as expressive as possible. I've made lovely friends through Toki Pona, and made art I'd never've been brave enough for otherwise. It's a lovely little home for my thoughts :)
