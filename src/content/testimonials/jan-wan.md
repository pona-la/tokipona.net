The language *toki pona* has always effortlessly interested me. Philosophy (such as Taoism) and meditation were peripheral hobbies, and *toki pona* united them with a creative fuel. In a world where too much information can cause overwhelm, speaking simply is a powerful way to get at the truth. Less nobly, it's been half a meditative tool, half a fun past time. Being a lurker in the community I've seen great wisdom being passed down as well as new ideas for this ever-changing lexicon. And honestly, something about a simple hieroglyphic language just scratches an itch.

I'm starting to interact with the community more and more and have even met REAL LIFE PEOPLE who speak it. But as of right now, *toki pona*'s biggest impact on me is its help with cutting away what isn't needed (*o weka*!) and focusing on what is important.
