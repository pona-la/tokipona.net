Toki Pona has helped me simplify my thoughts in a very cute way, that makes my depression easier to navigate. It’s a fun language to learn with interesting features and symbols, and its community can be very welcoming and creative. I use Toki Pona in my art on occasion because the fonts and tools the community has created are very useful when applied to art. Learning it is made easy with community resources and lessons!
