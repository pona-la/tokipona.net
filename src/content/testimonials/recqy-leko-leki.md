i... don't actually use toki pona all that often. it's a nice language to have around in my head, with all the silliness in the community 

(kijetesantakalu is very fun to be around) 

toki pona is a language, one that is quick and easy to pick up and get rolling (trust me you're gonna be thinking in and of this language all day) and it has definitely affected my life, not just in my own linguistic ability but also in my thoughts and feelings and how i learn about some things 

(as they say, "*mi ken ala toki pona e ijo la mi sona ala e ijo*")

and as for what toki pona means for me? as far as i can tell, i don't know, yet.
