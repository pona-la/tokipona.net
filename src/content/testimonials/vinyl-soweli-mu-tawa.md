Toki Pona was part of my personal recovery from being forcibly ejected out of an emotionally abusive relationship. It has changed the way i view language generally, and given me an eye for interesting oddities in the ways people speak.

Toki Pona is not „for“ anything. It is a language meant to convey meditative ideals on the nature of communication, written or verbal, but that does not define it, for it is still a language. I have aspired to use Toki Pona for conversing with the community, but find myself struggling to connect within the community. Most of my usage is in a personal diary.
