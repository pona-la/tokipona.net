Toki Pona has been a lens to focus my mind on what matters. I like how
ultimately the "shortcomings" one can have with the language are solvable with
more conversation. It encourages more communication. There are things one can
say with such eloquence and clarity that the minimal grammar allows; it's just
so wonderful. The coalescing of concepts that are really quite broad (looking at
you "_ko_") into a single word or indeed the lack of ability to convey certain
things (like "animal" in general rather than a specific sort of animal like
"mammal" or "reptile" etc) makes one reconsider the categories they put reality
into. This recategorization has helped in my journey to appreciating the Oneness
of God.
